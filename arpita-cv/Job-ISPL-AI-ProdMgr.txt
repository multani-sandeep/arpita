About the job
Job Description

AI Product Delivery Manager

Role Profile

Purpose of Role

The AI Product Delivery Manager coordinates the global rollout of ISP proprietary AI products for teaching and learning across ISP schools, ensuring purposeful and impactful delivery. This role focuses on project management, relationship building with school stakeholders, and operational execution across diverse educational environments. The role works closely with product, academic, and technical teams, owning the overall delivery plan for a set of ISP’s AI teaching and learning products.

ISP Principles

Begin with our children and students. Our children and students are at the heart of what we do. Simply, their success is our success. Wellbeing and safety are both essential for learners and learning. Therefore, we are consistent in identifying potential safeguarding and Health & Safety issues and acting and following up on all concerns appropriately.

Treat everyone with care and respect. We look after one another, embrace similarities and differences and promote the well-being of self and others.

Operate effectively. We focus relentlessly on the things that are most important and will make the most difference. We apply school policies and procedures and embody the shared ideas of our community.

Are financially responsible. We make financial choices carefully based on the needs of the children, students and our schools.

Learn continuously. Getting better is what drives us. We positively engage with personal and professional development and school improvement.

ISP AI Product Delivery Manager Key Responsibilities

 Lead phased adoption of allocated ISP proprietary AI teaching and learning products developed and piloted in LabSchools, tailoring rollout to adopter schools’ contexts.
 Oversee the global delivery schedule for AI products, including adoption, enablement, and alignment with school improvement plans.
 Maintain and update a live delivery tracker with key metrics such as adoption rates, training completion, and product usage.
 Prepare and present implementation dashboards and risk reports for governance groups and executive leadership.
 Manage day-to-day delivery operations of global rollout phases for AI teaching and learning products as instructed.
 Apply structured frameworks to build readiness and sustain behavioural change at school level in relation to AI-supported teaching and learning.
 Maintain trust and buy-in with school AI leads, EdTech leads, and regional stakeholders.
 Coordinate school readiness assessments to inform planning and deployment sequencing of AI products.
 Gather feedback from schools and ensure insights are shared with product, academic, and technical teams for responsive iteration and planning.
 Facilitate structured feedback loops and surface blockers, risks, and implementation insights.
 Act as a key liaison between school and regional academic, product, operational, and technical teams.
 Coordinate global CPD delivery for AI products through the Learning Hub and school-based sessions.
 Use operational and usage data to evaluate implementation progress, identify risks, and contribute to continuous improvement plans.
 Collaborate cross-functionally to ensure alignment between AI product delivery, pedagogy, and ISP priorities.

Experience

Skills, Qualifications and Experience

5+ years’ experience in complex global technology or education product delivery.
Experience gathering user feedback and responding to educator and school needs. 
Prior involvement in AI in education, learning technologies, or education innovation desirable.

Skills

Strong communication and stakeholder engagement skills.
Demonstrated ability to lead through influence in matrixed environments.
Confidence prioritising work and making evidence-informed trade-offs.
Deeply learner-focused and empathetic to teachers.
