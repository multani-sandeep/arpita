About the job
About the Role:

You will act as the voice of the customer, working closely with multiple engineering squads to define, prioritize, and deliver high-impact product capabilities.


Key Responsibilities:

Define and communicate the product vision and roadmap, aligned with business goals
Act as the voice of the customer, translating business needs into clear technical requirements
Own and manage the product backlog, ensuring priorities are well-defined and actionable
Work closely with 3–4 engineering squads to drive delivery and ensure alignment
Collaborate with Agile Delivery teams to support effective sprint and quarterly planning
Engage with stakeholders across product, engineering, and business teams
Track and analyze product performance metrics to guide decision-making
Maintain a strong understanding of the end-to-end platform ecosystem


Required Skills & Experience:

Proven experience in Product Management / Product Delivery / Technical Program Management
Strong ability to translate business requirements into technical solutions
Hands-on experience working with engineering teams in Agile environments
Solid understanding of modern software systems, APIs, and platform architecture
Experience with cloud platforms (AWS or Azure)
Exposure to real-time or low-latency data systems
Strong stakeholder management and communication skills
Data-driven mindset with experience using metrics for product decisions
