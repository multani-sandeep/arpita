Lead Delivery Manager

We're supporting a major digital transformation programme and are looking for a Lead Delivery Manager to play a key role in running delivery across a hub and spoke model.

This is a senior leadership role, focused on coordinating multiple workstreams across data, automation and wider technology transformation. You'll act as a central point for delivery teams, helping to drive alignment, accountability, and successful delivery in a complex programme environment.

This is a hybrid role requiring 2-3 days per week in London.

Responsibilities

Lead end-to-end delivery across multiple digital workstreams within a hub and spoke model
Coordinate cross-functional teams across data, automation and digital delivery
Manage Statements of Work, ensuring scope, deliverables and timelines are clearly defined and delivered
Track delivery against commercial and contractual commitments
Support the mobilisation and prioritisation of new workstreams in line with programme needs
Drive structure, momentum and accountability across a multi-stream delivery environment
Build strong relationships with internal and external stakeholders across the programme
Experience required

10-15 years' experience in digital delivery, programme management or consulting-led environments
Strong track record of leading complex, multi-stream digital programmes
Proven experience managing Statements of Work and commercial delivery
Good understanding of data, automation and digital engineering
Able to bring structure to complex delivery environments and drive teams towards outcomes
This is a strong opportunity to join a long-term engagement in a key lead role, with real responsibility for shaping delivery across a major transformation programme.
