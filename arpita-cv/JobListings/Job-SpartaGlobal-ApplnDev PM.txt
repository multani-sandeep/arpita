Sparta Global
Application Development Project Manager

About the job
This is a 12-Month FTC, requiring 3 days p/w on-site in London.

Overview:
Sparta Global are looking for an experienced Project Manager to oversee the delivery of multiple concurrent application development projects within a fast-paced technology environment.
This role requires someone who is highly organised, commercially aware, and confident managing several workstreams simultaneously while coordinating technical teams and stakeholders across the full project lifecycle.


Key Responsibilities:
Manage 4-5 concurrent application development projects simultaneously
Lead project delivery from initiation through to deployment and handover
Coordinate cross-functional technical teams including developers, QA, business analysts, and third-party suppliers
Build and maintain project plans, RAID logs, status reporting, and delivery documentation
Track project milestones, dependencies, budgets, and resource allocation
Facilitate Agile ceremonies and delivery governance where required
Manage stakeholder communication across both technical and non-technical audiences
Identify delivery risks and implement mitigation plans proactively
Ensure projects are delivered on time, within scope, and to agreed quality standards
Support continuous improvement of project delivery processes and governance standards


Required Experience:
Proven experience managing multiple concurrent IT or software development projects
Strong understanding of application development lifecycles and delivery methodologies
Experience working within Agile, Scrum, or hybrid delivery environments
Ability to manage competing priorities in a fast-moving environment
Strong stakeholder management and communication skills
Experience producing project reporting, delivery plans, and governance documentation
Confident working with both technical and business teams
