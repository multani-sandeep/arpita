Job Overview:

For our London office, we are seeking a Senior Agile Delivery Manager with extensive experience in delivering complex technology and systems transformation programmes, to support a high-profile project within the Media & Broadcasting sector.

Work Location: London, hybrid

Qualifications and Skills:

Around 8+ years’ experience in Agile Delivery Management, gained within technology or systems transformation programmes
Strong knowledge of Agile principles and ways of working, with the ability to pragmatically apply and adapt frameworks such as Scrum, Kanban, or equivalent within a programme context
Proven ability to support Discovery phases, including facilitating workshops, defining scope, and translating outputs into structured backlogs in collaboration with Project and Product Managers
Comfortable working embedded within a development team during Build and Close phases, providing hands-on delivery support and maintaining team cadence
Ability to translate team backlogs into plan views accessible to senior stakeholders, supporting programme reporting and bridging Agile delivery with programme-level governance
Experience in media, broadcast, or content technology environments is advantageous; understanding of content delivery workflows is beneficial
Scrum certification (or equivalent Agile certification)
