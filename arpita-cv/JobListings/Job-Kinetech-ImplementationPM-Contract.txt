About the job
Implementation Project Manager (Hands-On Tech Delivery)

6 Month Contract | Up to £375/day (Outside IR35)

Sporadic UK Wide Travel to client sites



This isn’t your typical Project Manager role.



If you enjoy sitting in meetings talking about RAID logs and governance frameworks - this probably isn’t for you.



If you prefer being on-site, solving real problems, working with suppliers, and actually getting tech live in busy environments - keep reading.



You’ll be working with a global hospitality business, delivering technology across stadiums, venues, universities and other large-scale environments. Think live events, high footfall sites, and situations where things need to work first time.



You’ll be right in the middle of it.



What you’ll be doing

You’ll take loosely defined ideas and turn them into something that works in the real world.

That means:

Speaking to stakeholders to understand what they actually need
Working with suppliers to figure out what’s possible
Keeping things moving across multiple projects at once
Being on-site for key moments - installs, go-lives, issue resolution
Getting stuck in when things don’t quite go to plan (because they won’t always)


It’s a mix of shaping solutions and delivering them - but the emphasis is very much on getting things done.



The kind of person this suits

You’ll likely but not necessarily be:

An Implementation PM, Deployment Manager, or Delivery-focused Project Manager
Used to working across multiple sites or locations
Comfortable dealing directly with suppliers and clients
Still close to the detail - not just managing from a distance


Experience in environments like hospitality, retail tech, venues, or similar will help.



Most importantly, you’re someone who:

Gets stuck in
Figures things out
Keeps things moving


Why this role

Exciting locations – stadia, events, large-scale venues
Variety – no two projects are the same
Real impact – you’ll see what you deliver actually being used
Flexibility – mix of site, home and office
6-month contract, outside IR35


If you’re looking for a role where you can just get on with it and make things happen, this is a good one.
