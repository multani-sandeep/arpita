About the job
Interim Project Manager

Location: Ealing (Hybrid working, with some office attendance required)
Day Rate: £Competitive (Outside IR35)
Contract: Interim (12 months)


Lead complex transformation projects that will help shape the future of our organisation

Are you an experienced Project Manager with a strong track record of delivering large-scale transformation programmes? Do you thrive in fast-paced environments where you can lead complex projects, influence senior stakeholders, and drive meaningful organisational change? If so, this could be the opportunity for you.


We’re looking for an Interim Project Manager to join our team, leading the delivery of multiple complex, organisation-wide projects that support strategic transformation and operational improvement across the business.


Key Responsibilities:

Lead the delivery of complex, high-impact transformation and change projects across the organisation 
Manage multiple interdependent workstreams, ensuring projects are delivered on time, within budget, and aligned to strategic objectives 
Develop integrated project plans, managing risks, dependencies, and delivery priorities effectively 
Establish and maintain strong governance, reporting, and project control processes 
Build strong relationships with senior stakeholders, influencing decision-making and driving accountability 
Lead cross-functional working groups and project teams to achieve successful delivery outcomes 
Monitor project budgets, delivery performance, risks, and benefits realisation 
Ensure business change is effectively embedded, working collaboratively with operational teams and stakeholders 
Produce high-quality reporting and insight for senior leadership and governance forums 
Drive continuous improvement and identify opportunities to enhance delivery, processes, and customer outcomes


More about you:

Significant experience delivering large-scale transformation, change, or operational improvement projects 
Prince2 Practitioner (or equivalent) qualification. 
Proven ability to manage complex projects with multiple stakeholders and competing priorities 
Strong experience managing project governance, risks, budgets, and delivery performance 
Excellent stakeholder management and influencing skills, including experience working with senior leaders 
Strong understanding of project delivery methodologies and change management principles 
Experience leading cross-functional teams and driving collaboration across business areas 
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to make informed decisions in fast-paced environments 
Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to convey complex information clearly and confidently 
Experience within housing, property, public sector, or regulated environments (desirable, not essential)/
