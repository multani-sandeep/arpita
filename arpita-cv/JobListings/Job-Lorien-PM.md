IT Project Manager - Retail Technology (Contract)
£500-£600/day (Inside IR35) | Hybrid (2-3 days per month in Watford) | 2-Year Programme
We are supporting a major retail transformation programme and looking for an experienced IT Project Manager to lead the delivery of next-generation in-store technology across a large retail estate. This is a long-term programme (2 years) focused on modernising store operations, mobile devices, and cloud-based services.
If you have strong delivery skills, thrive in fast-paced environments, and can drive cross-functional workstreams, this is a fantastic opportunity.
About the Role
You'll be responsible for delivering a range of store technology projects. Key areas of focus include:
Mobile & Store Technology Delivery

Managing the rollout and enhancement of Android-based mobile devices used across stores for scanning, markdowns, click & collect, and operational tasks.
Supporting the expansion of key store apps including task management, Outlook, ServiceNow, and Workday.
Leading initiatives to modernise existing solutions-such as rearchitecting from local server infrastructure to cloud-based architecture.

Cross-Functional Leadership

Working closely with Product Managers to map out project dependencies, priorities, and workstreams.
Feeding these into the scrum team to ensure smooth delivery and alignment.
Driving progress across multiple technical and business stakeholders.

Global Collaboration & Governance

Supporting the setup of a new offshore development team and establishing effective ways of working.
Providing clear, structured reporting into senior leadership.
Navigating a large, process-driven IT organisation with established governance frameworks.

What We're Looking For

Experience delivering technology projects within a large IT or enterprise environment.
Retail technology background is highly beneficial.
Experience working with offshore teams.
Strong stakeholder management with the ability to coordinate multiple workstreams.
Someone delivery-focused, proactive, and able to push projects forward.
Prior experience in similar retail organisations is a plus.
