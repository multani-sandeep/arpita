Role: Senior Project Manager, Retail Tech
Location: Remote

Role Overview:
We are hiring a Senior Project Manager with deep retail technology experience 
to lead delivery of digital and operational initiatives for Joveo's retail 
sector clients and partners. You will manage cross-functional teams, 
executive stakeholders, and technical delivery cycles in one of the most 
dynamic verticals in global hiring.

Key Responsibilities:
- Lead end-to-end delivery of retail technology projects on scope, schedule, and quality
- Manage stakeholder relationships across technical teams, operations, and client-side contacts
- Develop detailed project plans, resource allocations, and delivery timelines
- Drive risk identification, mitigation planning, and escalation protocols
- Coordinate with engineering, product, and integration teams on deliverables
- Produce executive-level reporting on project status, KPIs, and milestone progress

Required Skills & Qualifications:
- Proven experience managing technology projects within retail or e-commerce environments
- Strong command of project management methodologies (Agile, Waterfall, or hybrid)
- Ability to manage multiple concurrent workstreams in a fast-paced environment
- Excellent stakeholder communication skills across technical and non-technical audiences
- Proficiency with project management tools (Jira, MS Project, Smartsheet, or similar)
- PMP, PRINCE2, or equivalent certification is an advantage
