About the job
Senior Data Project Manager (Agile Delivery)



We are seeking a Senior Data Project Manager to lead the Agile delivery of complex, data-driven initiatives. This role is ideal for an experienced delivery leader with a strong background in data engineering and modern data platforms, who thrives in fast-paced, iterative environments.



Key Responsibilities:

Lead Agile delivery of data programmes, including data engineering, data platform, and analytics initiatives
Own and drive delivery across cross-functional squads (data engineers, analysts, architects, product owners)
Facilitate Agile ceremonies (sprint planning, stand-ups, retrospectives, backlog refinement) to ensure consistent, high-quality delivery
Partner with Product Owners to manage and prioritise backlogs aligned to business value
Remove delivery blockers, manage dependencies, and ensure continuous flow of value
Track progress using Agile metrics (velocity, burn-down/up, cycle time) and drive continuous improvement
Communicate effectively with senior stakeholders, translating technical delivery into business outcomes
Embed best practices in data governance, quality, and operational resilience within Agile delivery


Key Requirements:

Proven experience delivering data engineering or data platform projects in Agile environments
Strong understanding of modern data architectures (e.g. ETL/ELT pipelines, data lakes, cloud data platforms)
Hands-on experience with Agile frameworks (Scrum, Kanban, or scaled Agile)
Demonstrated ability to lead and coach cross-functional teams in Agile best practices
Excellent stakeholder management, facilitation, and communication skills
Comfortable working in iterative, fast-changing environments with evolving priorities


Desirable:

Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and modern data tooling
Agile certifications (e.g. Certified Scrum Master, SAFe)
Experience scaling Agile across multiple teams or programmes


Start date: We ideally need people that can start immediately



Rate: £550 per day, outside IR35



Location: The role is 100% remote but you must be UK based
