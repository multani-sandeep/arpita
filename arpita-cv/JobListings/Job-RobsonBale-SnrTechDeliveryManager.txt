About the job
Senior Technical Delivery Manager (Agile / SaaS / Multi-Squad Ownership)

Permanent | London (2-3 Days Per Week Onsite)


We are partnering with a large global technology organisation delivering cloud-based SaaS and bespoke software solutions to more than 20 million users worldwide.
As part of continued growth across Product and Engineering, they are looking for a Senior Technical Delivery Manager to lead complex engineering delivery across multiple squads within a large-scale, dependency-heavy environment.


Important

This role requires strong technical delivery expertise. 
While not a hands-on engineering role, you must be technically credible and comfortable working closely with Engineering Managers and Architects across complex software environments.


You should have a solid understanding of APIs, microservices, cloud platforms, CI/CD, technical dependencies, and the delivery challenges associated with large-scale engineering environments.
This role is focused on engineering delivery leadership rather than governance-led project management. 
Candidates whose experience is primarily centered around PMO coordination, milestone tracking, or governance-only delivery are unlikely to be a fit.


Reporting to the VP of Delivery, you will help drive execution maturity, delivery predictability, and cross-team coordination across product, platform, and operational initiatives.


Key Responsibilities

Own delivery across 3–4 engineering squads
Drive clarity around priorities, scope, sequencing, and execution
Manage delivery across feature work, technical debt, platform initiatives, and operational issues
Coordinate cross-team and transversal initiatives
Identify and manage delivery risks, dependencies, and technical constraints
Work closely with Engineering, Architecture, and Product teams
Improve delivery predictability, transparency, and execution maturity
Coach teams toward stronger collaboration, accountability, and delivery practices


What We’re Looking For

Significant experience within Technical Delivery, Agile Delivery, or Engineering Delivery environments
Experience managing delivery across multiple engineering squads
Proven delivery experience within SaaS or bespoke software environments
Strong understanding of modern software delivery practices and cloud-based systems
Experience managing complex dependencies and delivery risk
Ability to balance feature delivery against technical debt and platform stability
Strong stakeholder management and communication skills
Agile or Project Management certification (Scrum, SAFe, PRINCE2, PMP or equivalent)


This role will suit a senior delivery professional who combines strong execution leadership with genuine technical understanding and is comfortable operating close to engineering teams in complex software delivery environments.
