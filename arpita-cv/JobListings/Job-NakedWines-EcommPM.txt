About the job
In a nutshell, we're all about making the world of wine a better place. We fund and source directly from independent winemakers to bring customers better quality wine for a better price. No thirsty middlemen as far as the eye can see.

It's a different way of doing things, sure. But it works. We're one of the UK's favourite wine clubs, shipping over 1 million (!) cases a year to curious wine lovers. And with an ambitious road ahead, we've got no plans on plugging the cork on growth.

Our global team (we have offices in the US and Australia, too) is entrepreneurial by nature, obsessive about customer experience and driven by performance. All things that make Naked a great place to grow both personally and professionally. And yes, we like wine. A lot.

It takes a village to be Naked. That's why we're always on the lookout for eager talent to join the club. And now, our Digital team is looking for a Ecommerce Project Manager to join the fun on a fixed day rate contract basis. You'll act as a hands-on delivery leader, managing projects that improve our ecommerce experience, optimise conversion, and support international growth.

Working across product, engineering, marketing, data, and operations, you'll ensure projects are delivered efficiently, aligned to business goals, and centred around our customers and winemakers. So if you're keen to help us take the guesswork out of enjoying great wine, there's a Naked welcome pack with your name on it.

Together, we'll take Naked Wines to the next level - and share our not-so-well-kept secret with the world.

Requirements

Project Leadership: Own end-to-end delivery of high-impact ecommerce projects, including website enhancements, checkout optimisation, personalisation, and platform integrations

Growth & Optimisation: Support initiatives to improve conversion rate, customer retention, and lifetime value in collaboration with marketing and product teams
Scope & Planning: Define project scope, timelines, and dependencies. Build detailed project plans that balance delivery speed with high-quality outcomes
Technical Coordination: Manage integrations with third-party tools such as payment providers, personalisation platforms, and analytics suites
Risk Management: Proactively identify blockers and maintain a clear risk register with mitigation plans to keep delivery on track
Governance & Reporting: Provide structured updates on KPIs, progress, and risks, ensuring strong documentation and change control throughout the lifecycle
Stakeholder Partnership: Act as the central communication hub, aligning technical and non-technical stakeholders on priorities and rollout plans

Influential Communication: The ability to influence across cross-functional teams, navigate complex dependencies, and manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
