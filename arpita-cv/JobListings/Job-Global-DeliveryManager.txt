Job Description

Delivery Manager 

Reporting of the Role

This role typically reports to a Senior Delivery Manager or a Head of Delivery.

Overview Of Job

This role forms part of the Delivery function within Global’s Transformation team.
The Delivery function focuses on the delivery of Global’s Technology & Digital product roadmaps.
You will be responsible for creating and executing plans to deliver key Technology & Digital initiatives, building effective relationships with Product, Engineering and other business and technical stakeholders.
You will work closely with one or more squads to identify and resolve blockers and issues, identify and resolve dependencies and ensure delivery is on time and on budget.

3 Best Things About The Job
Being instrumental in the delivery of the technology roadmap for one of the most exciting transformation agendas in the media and entertainment market in the UK and beyond 
Working with stakeholders from across Global’s business to deliver value to Global’s audiences, advertisers and partners 
Having the authority to identify, coordinate and implement change and turn good ideas into reality, quickly 

Measures of success
In the first few months, you would have
Understood the inflight projects and the roadmap for your assigned area 
Gained a robust understanding of the operations of the business function(s) and supporting product(s) you will be working with 
Built effective working relationships and established clear responsibilities and accountabilities with the squad(s) you work with day-to-day 

Responsibilities Of The Role
Work alongside the Product Owner and Tech Lead in your squad(s) to understand the 'what and how' of the product roadmap 
Facilitate creation of the plan (the 'when') for the squad(s) and manage its execution, adapting rapidly as priorities shift or new tech capabilities emerge 
Work closely on a day-to-day basis with your squad(s) to ensure they are clear, unblocked and producing high quality outputs, leveraging AI tooling to accelerate delivery where appropriate 
Identify risks and issues within your squad(s) and mitigate, resolve or escalate as appropriate; proactively flag when legacy approaches may limit agility 
Understand inbound and outbound dependencies to deliver the plan; track and resolve these dependencies 
Respond to and resolve issues as required in product development, testing, deployment or post-launch, including live incidents 
Communicate clear summaries of progress against baseline plans and any changes, including root cause explanations, to other technology stakeholders 
Champion modern delivery processes, including rapid prototyping, hypothesis-driven development, and AI-assisted workflows; identify the most appropriate model(s) and implement improvements 
Contribute to a high-performance culture within your squad(s), with a clear sense of accountability for outcomes; celebrate success and experiment, iterate, and learn from failures 
Track, understand and communicate changes in squad throughput and performance using metrics, including AI-driven insights where valuable 

What You Will Need
Strong working knowledge of software development practices, methodologies and release lifecycles, with awareness of AI/ML development patterns (e.g., model training, versioning, deployment) 
Hands-on comfort with AI tooling (e.g., Claude, ChatGPT, automation platforms) to enhance your own productivity and support squad adoption 
Experience in supporting the creation of project plans and delivery of product roadmaps, including iterative approaches for emerging or experimental technology 
A focus on outcomes and business value, not process for process's sake 
An ability to prioritise tasks based on importance and urgency, re-prioritising confidently as technology or market conditions change 
An eye for detail and a willingness to understand the business context and technical content you are working with 
A keen ability to identify and resolve blockers and issues 
Emotional intelligence and empathy; a strong ability to build effective working relationships with both business and technical stakeholders through periods of disruption and change 
Effective communication skills, both verbally and in writing, ensuring squad members and other stakeholders are clear and seeking clarification where necessary 
Effective facilitation skills to provide direction, and maintain focus and momentum 

You’ll Be Someone Who….
Is energised by change and technology disruption, not daunted by it 
Is curious about AI and emerging tech, with a desire to learn how they can improve how we work 
Isn’t afraid of detail and wants to ‘understand the why’ 
Is a natural collaborator who enjoys working with a variety of teams, roles and people 
Is resilient and maintains a positive, solutions-focused attitude in the face of ambiguity, setbacks or rapid pivots 
Is flexible and adaptable, with an approach on outcomes; you understand the theory but can adapt to day-to-day reality 
Is comfortable operating in fast-changing, AI-influenced environments where best practice is still emerging 
Is comfortable leading through facilitation and building consensus, including with those who have greater experience and/or technical expertise
