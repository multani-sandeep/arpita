The Lead Project Manager, Professional Services is responsible for leading the successful execution of complex, strategic Professional Services engagements with enterprise customers through advanced project and program management leadership. This role manages large-scale initiatives involving multiple cross-functional stakeholders, strategic accounts, executive sponsors, and Authorized Partners. The Lead Project Manager drives alignment across business and technical teams, proactively manages risks and escalations, and ensures delivery outcomes align to customer business objectives and long-term value realization.

This individual serves as a trusted advisor to customers and internal stakeholders, influencing delivery strategy, operational excellence, and customer success outcomes across a portfolio of engagements. They will champion significant programs, enterprise initiatives, and organizational improvements using demonstrated leadership, creativity, and strategic thinking. This position is remote-friendly.

What You Bring
Strong executive presence with the ability to influence decision-making, communicate risk, and align stakeholders at all organizational levels. 
Ability to quickly learn, operationalize, and scale new processes, delivery models, and business initiatives. 
A proven strategic utility player who can adapt quickly and drive clarity in ambiguous environments with limited guidance. 
Strong customer relationship management skills with experience building trust across strategic and enterprise accounts. 
An innate curiosity and desire to continually improve operational efficiency, delivery quality, and customer outcomes. 
Demonstrated experience mentoring peers, influencing best practices, and contributing to organizational maturity. 
Relationship builder able to clearly communicate complex business and technical concepts to diverse audiences 

What You Will Do Day-to-day
Lead complex Professional Services delivery programs involving multiple concurrent workstreams, strategic customers, and Authorized Partners. 
Own and manage strategic account engagements, serving as a trusted advisor to customer stakeholders and internal leadership throughout the engagement lifecycle. 
Drive executive-level communication, stakeholder alignment, risk management, and escalation resolution for high-visibility customer initiatives. 
Partner closely with Professional Services leadership to align delivery execution with broader customer success and business objectives. 
Support Professional Services staff by owning and executing large-scale project and program plans through successful completion. 
Build and strengthen cross-functional relationships across the enterprise and with Authorized Partners to improve delivery coordination and customer outcomes. 
Facilitate complex requirements gathering and strategic planning sessions with cross-functional project teams, customers, and executive stakeholders.
Develop integrated roadmaps, governance models, timelines, and communication plans that drive accountability and realistic expectations. 
Identify opportunities to improve delivery processes, operational efficiency, customer experience, and organizational scalability. 
Conduct project and program post-mortems to review outcomes, capture lessons learned, and implement continuous improvement initiatives. 
Support organizational initiatives, operational programs, and additional responsibilities needed to drive team and business success 

What You Need To Succeed
5 years of functional experience with project management activities 
3 years of specific project management experience, either directly or as a responsibility of another role. 
PMP Certification preferred. 
Demonstrated experience managing strategic or enterprise customer engagements with executive-level stakeholders. 
Proven ability to lead across highly collaborative, cross-functional, and geographically distributed teams. 
Strong project and program management skills, including ownership of integrated project plans, dependencies, risks, issues, decisions, budgets, and stakeholder communications. Attention to detail is critical. 
Demonstrated ownership and accountability for results across the full end-to-end project and program lifecycle. 
Proven ability to influence without authority and drive alignment across technical, operational, and business teams. 
Experience navigating ambiguity and leading large initiatives with minimal supervision. 
Experience contributing to process improvement, operational maturity, or organizational transformation initiatives. 
Strong analytical, communication, and executive presentation skills
