Naked Wines
Ecommerce Change Manager (Contract)

In a nutshell, we're all about making the world of wine a better place. We fund and source directly from independent winemakers to bring customers better quality wine for a better price. No thirsty middlemen as far as the eye can see.

It's a different way of doing things, sure. But it works. We're one of the UK's favourite wine clubs, shipping over 1 million (!) cases a year to curious wine lovers. And with an ambitious road ahead, we've got no plans on plugging the cork on growth.

Our global team (we have offices in the US and Australia, too) is entrepreneurial by nature, obsessive about customer experience and driven by performance. All things that make Naked a great place to grow both personally and professionally. And yes, we like wine. A lot.

It takes a village to be Naked. That's why we're always on the lookout for eager talent to join the club. And now, our Digital Transformation team is looking for a Change Manager to join the fun on a fixed-day rate contract basis.

You will lead the "people side" of our major re-platforming initiative to Shopify, ensuring that new systems, structures, and processes are adopted smoothly to achieve our business goals. This is a key role where you'll drive our digital evolution: orchestrating change impact strategies and cross-functional readiness to ensure every roadmap milestone results in a flawless transition for our customers and our business.

Together, we'll take Naked Wines to the next level - and share our not-so-well-kept secret with the world.

Requirements

What you'll do

Roadmap Execution: Take ownership of the end-to-end change management plan, ensuring all activities are executed in strict alignment with Shopify project milestones
Impact Analysis: Lead detailed functional impact assessments, converting technical findings into high-impact communication and engagement workstreams
Business Readiness: Oversee recurring readiness audits to proactively identify friction points and certify that all teams are prepared for go-live
Workforce Transition: Accountable for the seamless transition of teams into new roles and ways of working, providing hands-on support for the rapid adoption of updated processes
Strategic Communication: Develop targeted engagement materials and orchestrate workshops and briefings to ensure transparent, two-way dialogue across the business
Training & Capability: Manage the full training lifecycle, from initial Needs Analysis through to the delivery of high-quality training content and technical documentation
Monitoring & Governance: Provide data-driven visibility into adoption rates and stakeholder sentiment, proactively identifying and mitigating project risks
Project Integration: Act as the primary bridge between change activities and the daily project lifecycle, partnering closely with Project Managers and workstream leads
What you'll bring

Professional Experience: 5+ years of dedicated Change Management experience, specifically leading transitions within complex ERP or Enterprise System environments
Methodology: Formally qualified in a recognised change methodology such as Prosci (ADKAR), ACMP (CCMP), or APMG Change Management
Technical Proficiency: Proven ability to translate technical system changes into functional user impacts; familiarity with Agile or Prince2 frameworks is highly desirable
Stakeholder Influence: Demonstrated success managing "Heads Of" and SLT-level stakeholders, navigating resistance and building consensus in high-pressure environments
Analytical Skills: Advanced capability in data-driven reporting, using metrics to track adoption and sentiment to inform strategic interventions
Communication Mastery: Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, with a portfolio of diverse engagement materials (presentations, briefings, and digital content)
Contractor Mindset: The ability to work at pace in a product-led environment, providing clear visibility on people-risks and transition readiness
You exhibit our Naked behaviours:

Ambition (dream big): implementing strategy into action and providing feedback (what works and doesn't) 
Judgement (make good decisions): questioning the status quo, testing hypotheses and sharing outcomes
Discipline (adhere to high standards): delivering on commitments, on time with quality
Influence (have a big impact): collaborating effectively, actively working on own development
Accountability (take full responsibility): delivering results: recognising mistakes and quickly shares them
