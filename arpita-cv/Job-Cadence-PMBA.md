Project Manager/Business Analyst - Technology Transformation

Project Manager, Technology Transformation, Technology Outsourcing, Organisational Change, Third party management

Experienced, hands-on PM/BA required to join a fast-paced global Technology organisation as part of a high-profile Transformation Programme. This is a varied role where you'll draw on both project management and business analysis skills to drive real, meaningful change.

You'll be project managing key workstreams within a broader Transformation Program, managing and interpreting data sets to inform decisions, and building and maintaining project plans, RAID logs and program reporting. Working closely with the Programme Manager, Transformation Leads and wider stakeholders, you'll also be responsible for engaging third party MSPs and managing associated dependencies.

The role requires extensive PM/BA or hybrid experience, with a strong background in organisational change delivery within a Technology function. Proven experience in technology outsourcing, restructuring and/or the reorganisation of technology functions is essential, as is experience working with and managing third party MSPs. You'll bring strong stakeholder management skills across all levels, the ability to work independently and structure your own workload, and the confidence to juggle multiple priorities whilst operating in a high pressure, fast-paced environment.

This exciting opportunity is offered with flexible hybrid working.
