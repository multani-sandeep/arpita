## PROSCI Certification - What to Do and When

**PROSCI vs ADKAR - clarification**
- ADKAR is not a separate certification - it is PROSCI's model (Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement)
- The real choice is PROSCI vs APMG Change Management Practitioner

**PROSCI vs APMG - which one**
- PROSCI: £2,800-£3,300, globally dominant, 100,000+ certified, most cited in JDs, preferred by corporate and technology transformation employers including Deloitte
- APMG: from £1,300, stronger in UK/Europe public sector, Foundation + Practitioner levels
- For target market (retail, FinServ, technology transformation): PROSCI is the stronger signal; APMG is only the right choice for public sector roles

**Employer demand data**
- ~30% of change management JDs specifically request certification; 70% do not - but certification unlocks the premium salary band
- When certification is named in a JD, PROSCI is the most cited - typically worded "change management certification e.g. PROSCI, APMG Change Practitioner"
- 20-35% of roles now require or strongly prefer PROSCI - nearly doubled in recent years
- Roles citing PROSCI skew toward technology transformation, enterprise digital and AI adoption - directly in the target space

**How PROSCI certification works**
- 3-day intensive programme, online (instructor-led) or in-person
- Certification exam on day 3; covers ADKAR model + PROSCI 3-Phase Process
- No prerequisite qualifications needed
- Outcome: PROSCI Certified Change Practitioner
- Book via uk-store.prosci.com

**LinkedIn Learning - what to cover now (free with Premium)**
- Change Management Foundations - ADKAR model, Kotter 8-step, core frameworks
- Leading Organisational Change - stakeholder engagement, resistance handling
- Change Management for Projects - PM + change integration, directly maps to existing background
- Communicating Change - communication planning, sponsorship model
- Managing Organisational Change - impact assessment, readiness measurement
- Complete in first 6-8 weeks; add completions to LinkedIn profile; builds vocabulary to apply in Royal Mail role immediately

**Sequencing**
- Now to August 2026: LinkedIn Learning - build ADKAR and change vocabulary; apply frameworks explicitly at Royal Mail
- September to December 2026: PROSCI certification - invest once 3-4 months of Royal Mail change evidence exists; the programme requires applying the methodology to a real change initiative - Royal Mail is the ideal case study
- January 2027 onwards: CV refresh with PROSCI + Royal Mail change framing; begin search ahead of March 2027 contract end

## PROSCI vs APMG - Full Comparison

**Conceptual - what each teaches**

| | PROSCI | APMG |
|---|---|---|
| Framework | Single model: ADKAR (5 stages of individual change) + PCT (Project Change Triangle) | Multi-framework toolkit based on Change Management Body of Knowledge - no single prescribed model |
| Approach | Prescriptive and practical - one method applied deeply | Broad and theoretical - many models (Kotter, Lewin, McKinsey 7-S), you choose what to apply |
| Format | 3-day experiential workshop; build a real change plan during the programme | Foundation exam (theory) then Practitioner exam (case scenarios) - study and exam cycle |
| Toolkit | Templates, assessments and tools included | No standard templates; teaches concepts, you build your own tools |
| Best fit for | Technology and systems change with a fixed implementation date - directly matches Royal Mail, Worldpay ServiceNow, Copilot rollout type work | Broader organisational change, consulting, enterprise transformation theory |

**Job demand - what employers ask for**

| | PROSCI | APMG |
|---|---|---|
| JD mention | Most cited globally when certification is specified; dominant in US, growing fast in UK corporate | More common in UK/European public sector and some consulting firms |
| Salary band | £82k-£117k premium band data is tied specifically to PROSCI; Deloitte and major tech firms have built elevated bands for PROSCI-certified practitioners | No equivalent elevated salary band data linked to APMG alone |
| Target sectors | Technology transformation, FinServ, retail, global corporate - the primary target market | Public sector, UK/Europe consulting, NHS, local government |
| JD wording | "PROSCI certification preferred" or "e.g. PROSCI, APMG" - PROSCI named first | Mentioned as an acceptable alternative, rarely named first |
| Global recognition | 100,000+ certified; truly global | Strong UK/Europe; limited US recognition |

**Direct answer: does APMG unlock the higher salary band?**
- APMG gets you into change management conversations at the level you already compete at as a PM - around £65-75k
- It signals intent but does not unlock the £80-90k P&C Change Manager band - that premium is specifically tied to PROSCI in the market data
- APMG at £1,300 is worth it only to signal change management intent while saving for PROSCI; it will not move the pay needle on its own
- Exception: public sector change roles - APMG is equally credible there, but public sector change salaries are lower (£53-63k range)

## Where to Buy PROSCI - Cheaper Provider Options

PROSCI licences third-party organisations to deliver the same certification. These are cheaper than booking directly from PROSCI UK.

**Direct PROSCI (most expensive)**
- PROSCI UK Store (direct): https://uk-store.prosci.com/training.html - approximately £3,075
- PROSCI Europe (direct, virtual): https://www.proscieurope.co.uk/en/prosci-change-management-practitioner-certification-programme

**Third-party licensed providers (cheaper - same certification)**
- Change Partners (Estonia, delivers online, best price found): https://www.changepartners.ee/en/prosci-certification/ - €2,290 (~£1,950)
- PROSCI EU Store (virtual cohorts, sometimes cheaper than UK direct): https://eu-store.prosci.com

**Check regularly for pricing and cohort dates** - prices vary by cohort and region; online delivery from a European provider gives the same PROSCI digital badge as UK in-person.

**Can I self-study and just take the exam?**
- No - PROSCI does not offer a standalone exam or self-study path; this is by design
- The 3-day instructor-led programme IS the certification; the day 3 exam is part of the programme, not a separate product
- There is no way to buy study materials and sit an exam independently
- The certification that does work this way is APMG (Foundation + Practitioner are exam-based and self-study is possible) - but APMG alone does not unlock the higher salary band
- No cheap shortcut exists for the PROSCI badge; programme attendance is mandatory
