# Reskilling Plan: Leadership & Governance
**Target Timeline:** 4 Weeks
**Objective:** Master key competencies in AI Leadership, Data Governance, Transformation Governance, and Change Leadership to pivot into a strategic leadership role.

> Use `Tracker.md` to track your daily progress and `Visibility.md` to share your insights.

---

## 1. Strategic AI Leadership
**Focus:** Moving beyond the hype to understanding AI as a strategic business enabler.

*   **Skills & Knowledge:** AI strategy & adoption planning, AI-driven decision-making, Evaluating AI ROI, Ethical AI oversight.
*   **Frameworks & Tools:** AI Adoption Frameworks, Responsible AI Guidelines, AI Governance Models.
*   **Soundbytes to Master:**
    *   "Driving value from AI without coding."
    *   "AI as a strategic enabler, not just a tool."
    *   "Embedding AI in decision-making and governance."

## 4. Change Leadership
**Focus:** Leading humans through uncertainty and embedding new cultures.

*   **Skills & Knowledge:** Leading through ambiguity, Stakeholder engagement, Resistance management, Change impact assessment.
*   **Frameworks & Tools:** Kotter 8-Step Model, Prosci ADKAR, McKinsey 7S.
*   **Soundbytes to Master:**
    *   "Influencing change without authority."
    *   "Embedding AI/data culture across the organisation."
    *   "Guiding stakeholders through uncertainty."
