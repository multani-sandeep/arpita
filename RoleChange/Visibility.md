# Visibility & Personal Branding Plan

**Goal:** Create authentic visibility on LinkedIn and Substack by sharing the reskilling journey.
**Strategy:** "Learn, Do, Teach." Share insights as you learn them, not just after you master them.

## 📣 Content Strategy: The "Builder's Journey"
Instead of acting like an expert immediately, position yourself as a **strategic leader who is constantly evolving**.
*   **Hook:** "I'm digging deep into [Topic] this week..."
*   **Insight:** "Most people think X, but I realized Y..."
*   **Action:** "Here is a simple framework I found useful..."
*   **Engagement:** "How do you handle this in your org?"
