## Allegations Raised by West Herts CC (WHCC)
1. **Threat to Saif Azad (16, WHCC junior)** — alleged Bushey player Hamza Wahid said words to the effect of "I will come after you" plus expletives, while WHCC was batting. Saif's own email account and two parent witness statements were submitted in support.
2. **Threat via captain-to-captain exchange** — WHCC captain Dillon Mahawatte alleges Bushey captain Para Sivatharman said, "Just wait. When your youngsters come out to bat, I'm going to get my adults to have a go at them," referring to WHCC junior players. Said to have been witnessed by Prad Kanuga.
3. **Witness statement (Umang Buch)** — describes Hamza directing a threat ("I'll come and get you after the game") at an unnamed 15-year-old player near the boundary; states the child was distressed and was removed from the ground before the match ended as a precaution.
4. **Umpire's report (Prad Kanuga)** — details four in-match incidents:
   - Boundary call at ball 5.3 disputed by Bushey players/captain, reduced from 4 to 3 runs under sustained pressure.
   - Abuse following a wide call at ball 12.2 (sworn at, called "cheat"/"prick").
   - No-ball called for an alleged illegal bowling action (S. Ahmed) at ball 21.6, met with dissent.
   - Bushey players allegedly surrounded and intimidated the 15-year-old square-leg umpire (a WHCC junior volunteer) after he corroborated the no-ball call.
   - The umpire's report separately states **three distinct safeguarding concerns regarding Para Sivatharman's conduct will be submitted separately** — not yet in this folder; follow up to obtain.

## Bushey CC's Response (Para Sivatharman, 29 June 2026 email)
- **Denies** saying "I'm going to get my adults to have a go at them." States his actual comment was an "advisory, light-hearted warning" made after WHCC juniors (Sachin Kanuga, Kumaran Thangiah) sledged Bushey's senior batters and WHCC's captain declined to intervene.
- **Denies** calling the umpire a "cheat," but accepts he questioned the umpire's honesty over the boundary call, citing a fielder's (Arshad Malik) account that the ball did not cross the line.
- **Disputes** the characterisation of the square-leg umpire exchange — says he asked the junior umpire to explain the no-ball call, and denies intimidation.
- **Raises counter-concerns**: alleges WHCC player Prad Kanuga (umpire, and parent of WHCC's keeper) repeatedly targeted two Bushey juniors — Shay Solanki (14, mocked while batting/bowling, reportedly upset) and Saad (junior bowler, alleges Prad had "hands on Saad's shoulders" while he bowled).
- States a separate statement from Hamza Wahid was prepared in response to the allegation against him (referenced as attached, not present in this folder — follow up to obtain).
