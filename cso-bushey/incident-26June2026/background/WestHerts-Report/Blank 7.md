# Witness Statement

**Name of Witness:** Umang Buch
**Role/Position:** Parent of the player in Playing XI
**Date of Incident:** 27/06/
**Location:** King George Field
**Match:** Bushey 3rd XI vs West Herts 5th XI

**Statement**

I am providing this statement regarding an incident that
occurred during the above cricket match involving an adult
player, Hamza, and a 15-year-old participant.

During the match, Hamza approached the boundary area
where the 15-year-old player was present. While near the
boundary, Hamza directed threatening and intimidating
comments towards the child. Specifically, he stated words
to the effect of, **“I’ll come and get you after the game.”**

The comment was made by an adult towards a minor and
was perceived as a threat. The young player appeared
distressed and concerned by the remark. The behaviour was
inappropriate and raised immediate safeguarding concerns
for the welfare and safety of the child.

Due to the nature of the threat and the potential risk to the
young player, those responsible for the child’s welfare took
precautions to ensure his safety. As a result, arrangements
were made for the 15-year-old to leave the ground before
the conclusion of the match to avoid any further
confrontation or risk of harm.


In my view, the conduct displayed by Hamza was
unacceptable and contrary to the principles of safeguarding
young people in cricket. All players, officials, and
spectators have a responsibility to provide a safe and
respectful environment for children participating in sport.
Threatening language directed by an adult towards a minor
is inconsistent with these responsibilities and caused
significant concern at the time of the incident.

I believe the above account to be true and accurate to the
best of my knowledge and recollection.

**Name:** Umang Buch
