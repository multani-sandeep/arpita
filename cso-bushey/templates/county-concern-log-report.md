# Club Safeguarding Officer → County Notification Template

Use this to notify the County Safeguarding Officer that a concern has been logged at club level. This is a **notification of a logged concern**, not a full incident report — attach the full referral form (Low Level / Threshold) separately per Herts Cricket / ECB Safe Hands process.

**To:** Julie Page, Hertfordshire County Safeguarding Officer — safeguarding@hertscricket.org
**Cc:** Deputy County Safeguarding Officer (Dominic Chatfield), Club Chair
**From:** [Your name], Club Safeguarding Officer, [Club name]
**Date sent:** [DD/MM/YYYY]

---

**Subject:** Safeguarding concern logged — [Club name] — [short description, e.g. "27 June fixture v West Herts CC"]

Dear Julie,

I am writing in my capacity as Club Safeguarding Officer for [Club name] to notify you that a safeguarding concern has been logged at club level, in line with ECB Safe Hands procedure.

**Date concern was raised to me:** [DD/MM/YYYY]
**Date/location of the incident (if applicable):** [DD/MM/YYYY, ground/venue]
**Reported by:** [name/role — e.g. opposing club chair, parent, umpire]
**Person(s) the concern relates to:** [name(s), age if a child, role — player/official/volunteer/parent]
**Nature of concern:** [one or two sentences — factual, no judgement on outcome. E.g. "Allegation of threatening language directed at a minor during a match."]
**Category (if known):** ☐ Low Level Concern ☐ Threshold

**Immediate actions taken by the club so far:**
- [e.g. Concern logged and dated]
- [e.g. Welfare check made with the young person(s)/parent(s) involved]
- [e.g. Interim arrangements made for any future fixtures between the parties]

**Documents held on file (available on request):** [list — e.g. witness statement, umpire report, email correspondence]

**Support/guidance requested from County:** [e.g. confirmation this should be treated as Threshold; guidance on next steps; whether County will lead any further investigation]

I confirm this concern is being kept on a confidential, access-limited club safeguarding record. Please let me know if you require the full referral form or supporting documents ahead of this being sent.

Kind regards,
[Your name]
Club Safeguarding Officer, [Club name]
[Phone] / [Email]

---

## Notes for use
- Send this **as soon as the concern is logged** — don't wait for the underlying facts to be resolved or for both sides' accounts to be reconciled.
- Keep this notification factual and free of your own judgement on who is right; let County/League determine outcome.
- If the concern involves an immediate risk to a child, do not wait to send this — call the NSPCC helpline (0808 800 5000) or police (999) first, then notify County.
