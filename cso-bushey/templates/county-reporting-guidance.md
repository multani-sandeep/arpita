## Cricket Safeguarding Concern Reporting – Guidance



# 1. What is the context of concern?

**Purpose:** Where and in what situation did this happen?

**Look for:**

- Match / training / social setting / online / travel / accommodation
- Club, league, academy, or pathway involvement
- Dates, times, locations
- Who was present (roles, not opinions)

**Answer format:**

“The concern relates to [activity/event] involving [team/club/individual] at [location/date].”

# 2. What is the type of concern?

**Purpose:** Categorise the issue clearly

Use consistent categories such as:

- Low Level / Threshold concern (category)
- Safeguarding (adult at risk / child protection)
- Conduct / behaviour
- Discrimination
- Poor practice
- Bullying / harassment
- Integrity (e.g. corruption, gambling)
- Health & safety
- Welfare / wellbeing concern

**Answer format:**

“This appears to be a [category] concern involving [brief summary].”

# 3. Please enter the details of the concern

**Purpose:** The factual narrative – THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT PART

**Include:**

- What exactly happened (chronological order)

- Who was involved (names, contact details + roles where known)
- What was said/done (use neutral language)
- Any witnesses
- Evidence (messages, photos, reports)
- Whether this is first-hand or reported information

**DO:**

- Stick to facts
- Use direct quotes if helpful
- Be objective (avoid assumptions)

**DON’T:**

- Add opinions (“this was inappropriate” → instead describe behaviour)

**Answer format:**

“On [date], it is reported that [individual]...
The incident involved...
Witnesses include...
Supporting evidence includes...”

# 4. What action(s) have been taken?

**Purpose:** Show what has already been done

**Look for:**

- Was the issue reported already?
- Immediate safety actions taken?
- Who has been informed? (Club/ League Safeguarding Officer, police, ECB, etc.)
- Has the person who the concern has been made about been informed
- Is the subject of the concern under the age of 18? If yes, have their
    parent(s)/guardian(s)/carer(s) been informed?
- Any interim measures (suspension, supervision, separation)

**Answer format:**

“The concern has been reported to...
Immediate actions taken include...
No further action has yet been taken / The matter is ongoing.”
